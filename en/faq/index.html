Do you also work at night or on weekends?

Yes, for emergency interventions we are also available outside office hours. Call our number and we'll immediately discuss what we can do.

How much does an emergency intervention cost approximately?

The price depends on the problem, the time and any parts required. We always discuss the cost upfront, so you're never faced with surprises. Feel free to ask for an estimate over the phone.

In which area do you work?

We are active in Flemish Brabant, the Brussels Capital Region and Walloon Brabant. Not sure if your municipality is included? Give us a call and we'll check.

Can you bring an outdated fuse box up to standard for inspection?

Yes. We renew or expand your distribution panel according to the AREI/RGIE, so the installation meets current standards and is ready for inspection.

Do you also work for businesses and commercial premises?

Yes, in addition to private clients we also serve offices, shops and SMEs. We work around your opening hours to minimise the impact on your business.

Do you provide a guarantee on completed work?

Yes, all completed work and supplied materials are covered by the legal guarantee. For specific installations, we discuss the exact conditions during the quote.

What is the difference between an RCD and a circuit breaker?

A circuit breaker protects against short circuits and circuit overloads. An RCD protects people: it trips in the event of a leakage current. Both are essential in a modern installation.

Can you install an EV charging point for my electric car?

Yes, we handle the electrical connection of charging points, including adapting your fuse box if necessary for the additional load.
